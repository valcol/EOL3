Why this redesign?

We became increasingly frustrated with EOL as it is right now. We see it as a rather fixed, very bureaucratic entity which isn't very flexible and agile when it comes to design and usability. We want EOL to be better, so that it can become truly useful for everyone. That's why we've decided to propose this redesign, EOL3

Planned roll-out

If we can't get EOL to change, we will raise money to build an alternative version based on our EOL3 concept. Make sure to check us out on Facebook and Twitter to get the latest updates!
