Inventorying, faster

EOL3's goal is to make taxonomists' job easier. By giving them the information they need, from references to virtual specimens for identifications, they can discover and describe new species faster. Estimates put the total number of species on Earth at at least 10 million, of which we've only discovered 2 million. Countless species remain to be discovered - and many of them go extinct before they have a chance to be inventoried -. EOL3 wants to become the reference taxonomists use to identify and describe new species.

Universal access to knowledge

EOL3 is dedicated to Open Science. At the core of EOL3's mission is the desire to make knowledge accessible to everyone through the internet. By doing so we believe people can educate themselves, make better decisions, and become aware of nature's beauty and frailty.
